Welcome

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio page. With six years of information technology experience I decided it was to time to explore other skills. I was so excited to begin my journey into programming. It has been challenging but highly enjoyable learning and practicing coding. My goal is to contribute to society in a positive way that helps in the aid of others.

My father is pre-diabetic and with the advancement in technology/development my father and others like him no longer have to prick their fingers several times a day. Software development and technology are responsible for a contactless blood sugar test. I couldn't think of better reason to begin my journey into software development. My years in system administration and cyber security came with challenges that required critical thinking and efficient problem solving. Software development requires many of the skills used in information technology when it comes to findng solutions to problems that generate from implementation. My passion for technology has led me down a satisfying path and I can't wait to see where it takes me next.
